4C™ – Key Benefits
Saves money by tracking and preventing failures
Proactive monitoring allows prevention of failures. Early detection of exceptional situations, including possible fraudulent activities and misconduct, helps to run business flawlessly.
Saves time by solving problems faster and with less effort
Improved response is achieved by receiving pinpointed information before or right after the incident occurrence. Root-Cause analysis reduces an effort by pointing to the root of the problem.
Real-time monitoring
System monitoring status is updated in real-time (scheduling is also available). Immediate notifications are sent via SMS and email. Problem can be traced and fixed in a matter of minutes.
Self-improvement through the wisdom of the crowds
Skywind 4C™ customers develop their own alerts. Each new alert is available to all other customers at once.
Best Practices – predefined out-of-the-box alert templates
No need to waste time deciding how or what to capture – start with our predefined alerting templates. They cover business processes, infrastructure functioning and applications Alert library grows constantly and new alerts are included in Skywind 4C™ license.
Custom, self-service alerts and reports creation with Skywind 4C™ Alert Generator
In a matter of minutes, users can generate custom based alerts and repports, based on their own ABAP or HANA entities – no technical proficiency required.
Showing customer his unique pain points
Extensive benchmark of the customers with profile, similar to that of your company, allows us recommend for the alerts, most appropriate to your industry and lines of business.
Incidents are managed as tasks
Manage your incidents as tasks, constantly measuring the rate of problem’s solving
Easy to use – self service with no need to wait for IT
Business people have to be informed. They can quickly and easily register their own alerts, trace processes with ultimate precision.
User friendly and self-explanatory
Intuitive and adaptive interface requires just a few minutes to learn how to use the system. Clean and simple look, minimal number of clicks, shortest possible path to each function.
All monitoring data is available from one place. Monitoring and management using Skywind 4C™ is much easier than using traditional SAP tools.
Adaptive and accessible anywhere
4C™ runs smoothly on any smart phone or web browser due to its HTML5 front end interface.
Low installation costs
Only minimal installation time is required. No additional hardware expenses since 4C™ is cloud-based and easily scalable.
