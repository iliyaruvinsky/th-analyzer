4C™ – Overview
Skywind 4C™ is the first self-service, real-time cloud based
Fraud Detection
,
Business Performance
, and
Technical Inconsistencies
monitoring platform to control all SAP environments.
Skywind 4C™ provides executives, business users and IT teams with constant flow of vital information about condition of the processes, running in SAP environment.
Skywind 4C™ helps to optimize, accelerate and facilitate people’s daily routines via precise and instant alerting and reporting about mission critical issues.
Using Skywind 4C™ Cloud, SaaS, Mobile, SAP UI5 solution, customers increase the level of business and technical control, over their SAP domain.
Skywind 4C™ alert catalog contains hundreds of highly customizable alerts and reports, categorized and organized into packages. Each package covers specific area of usage, allowing controlling the following aspects of SAP operations:
FRAUD DETECTION
(Procurement, Finance, Sales, Controlling etc.)
BUSINESS PROCESSES
(SD/MM/HCM/FI/CO/PM/QM)
INFRASTRUCTURE
(BASIS/DB/Communications)
APPLICATIONS
(BW/Security/User Management)
Skywind 4C™ provides users with monitoring and analysis of real-time events and historical inconsistencies via modern centralized interface with no need to use numerous transactions and screens.
With Skywind 4C™ you can notify anyone about any SAP event via email or text message. Using Skywind 4C™Alerting Cockpit, it is easy to track and observe all events at a single screen. Being a completely SaaS platform, Skywind 4C™ can be deployed in minutes and become fully operational in a matter of hours – no hardware or software is required.
Business impact
: Skywind 4C™ deployment prevents unplanned expenses providing contextual real-time and historical business insights based on your corporate SAP data. E.g. power-users are immediately informed when erroneous business events or infrastructure malfunctions occur. Skywind 4C™ has extensive fraud detection capabilities – it can catch events that might be considered as fraud: vendor’s data manipulations, exceptional sales events, purchasing misconducts etc.
