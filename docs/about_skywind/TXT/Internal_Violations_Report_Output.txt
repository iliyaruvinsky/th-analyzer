vs. T-Code

Filters with values, explicitly entered by a user, can be shown in detail by clicking on a folder icon inside the filter and then can be exported to Excel. Filters that were not explicitly applied, are marked with a
“*”
sign (all values) and are not clickable.
To display detailed data about previously set filters, you can click on a chosen filter and the popover will be displayed:
The lower block of this screen presents the report’s output. The table displays users’ details, conflicting T-Codes, found via SoD Rules. To navigate an manage f the output, display field names and filter by Critical Objects use the buttons in the toolbar above the table, use table’s header to filter, export to PDF and Excel using the buttons at the frame footer:
When you click the
<Export to Excel>
button, all the report data will be exported to a spreadsheet (.xlsx file).
When you click the
<Export to PDF>
button a popover will be displayed. Here you can choose which fields to export, to avoid exporting less important information to a printable PDF file. To do so, you can uncheck unnecessary fields using the checkboxes:
You can display a full report’s row in a popover by clicking on the leftmost column icon against the desired report row. It is convenient to look at all the data without scrolling the footer scroll bar.  You can also export the presented data to Excel:
You can check where else roles, rules and T-Codes are used by clicking the links in
Role, Rule, T-Code
and
vs T-Code
columns:
