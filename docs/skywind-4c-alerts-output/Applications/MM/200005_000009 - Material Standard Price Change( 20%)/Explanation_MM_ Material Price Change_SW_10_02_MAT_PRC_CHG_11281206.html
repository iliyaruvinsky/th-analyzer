MM: Material Price Change (SW_10_02_MAT_PRC_CHG)

General

This exception indicator allows calculation of the difference between an old price and a new price in absolute value and percentages (absolute value) with the possibility to filter by percentage change.
In order to examine possible changes, a comparison is made for the following field pairs :
PEINH VS PEINH_ NEW - price unit PVPRS_OLD VS PVPRS_NEW - moving average price STPRS_OLD VS STPRS_NEW - standard price VPRSV_OLD VS VPRSV_NEW - price control indicator .

Parameters

Special paramemters:
DATE_REF_FLD - DATE reference field. This parameter can get one of 2 values: Day On Which Accounting Document Was Entered (CPUDT) or Document Date(BLDAT). Default value is Day On Which Accounting Document Was Entered .
BACKDAYS calculated according to DATE_REF_FLD parameter.
